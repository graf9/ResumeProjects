Glossary of Acronyms

  • UNRATE: Unemployment Rate – the percentage of the labor force that is jobless.
  • GDPC1: Real Gross Domestic Product – the total value of goods and services produced, adjusted for inflation (in trillions for this dashboard).
  • CPIAUCSL: Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers – a measure of price changes for a basket of goods and services.
  • FEDFUNDS: Federal Funds Rate – the overnight rate at which banks lend to each other.
  • INDPRO: Industrial Production Index – a measure of the output of the industrial sector.
  • PCE: Personal Consumption Expenditures – a measure of consumer spending.
  • M2SL: M2 Money Stock – a broad measure of the U.S. money supply.

How I Built This Dashboard:
I built this dashboard using Python, Flask, Plotly, and Pandas to fetch, process, and visualize economic data from the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) API. The dashboard dynamically displays key economic indicators through interactive charts and summary metrics.

Technologies & Tools: Python, Flask, Plotly, Pandas, HTML, CSS, and the FRED API. Explore the source code and documentation on my GitHub repository.
