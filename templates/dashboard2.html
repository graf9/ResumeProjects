AI-Powered Economic Forecast Dashboard

How This Works: This dashboard retrieves real-world economic data from the Federal Reserve Economic Database (FRED) and applies **AI-based predictive modeling** (Prophet) to forecast trends for key economic indicators. It evaluates performance using **Mean Absolute Error (MAE) and Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)** for accuracy assessment.

Note: Every time you click "Get Forecast," the app fetches the latest data, trains AI models, and generates forecasts. This process may take up to **20 seconds**.
